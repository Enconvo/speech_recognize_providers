## Nvidia Parakeet

**POST** `speech_recognize_providers/nvidia_parakeet`

**POST** `speech_recognize_providers/qwen`

Alibaba's multilingual ASR powered by Qwen3-ASR, supporting language identification and speech recognition for 30 languages and 22 Chinese dialects, with state-of-the-art accuracy among open-source ASR models
